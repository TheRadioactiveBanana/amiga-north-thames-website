Amiga North Thames is a computer user group, based in North East London (U.K.) which was started back in January 1999. We are a dedicated bunch of enthusiasts who fondly remember the Amiga and other systems from its day.

We meet up once a month and discuss, demonstrate, build, game, and repair our old machines.
We also arrange head-to-head or multi-player gaming sessions this could be from anything from Stunt Car Racer, Sensible Soccer, and Skidmarks right up to Freespace 2 and beyond.

Got a Classic Amiga and want to upgrade or repair it?
Want to try or install the latest CaffeineOS or AmigaOS?
Want to show off your latest build or work on something together?
Whatever it is, bring it along and set it up!

Amiga North Thames remains the U.K.'s longest running and most successful Amiga enthusiast group covering London and the South-East. We offer a warm welcome to anybody who prefers computing with all the fun that Windows took away. Admittance is free for your first meeting, thereafter it is £8.00 per meeting. Meetings start at 13:00 and last 4 hours.
There is no membership fee.
